About Our CAV Research

Our research focuses on developing next-generation connected and automated vehicle technologies that address real-world transportation challenges.

Our Mission

To develop innovative connected and automated vehicle technologies that enhance safety, efficiency, and sustainability in transportation systems through cutting-edge research and real-world applications.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in connected and automated vehicle research, contributing to the development of intelligent transportation systems that transform urban mobility and create safer roads for all users.

Our Approach

We combine theoretical research with practical implementation, using advanced simulation, machine learning, and real-world testing to develop robust solutions for connected vehicle ecosystems.

Research Facilities

State-of-the-art facilities and equipment supporting advanced CAV research and development.

Simulation Laboratory

Advanced traffic simulation environment with SUMO, VISSIM, and custom-built simulation frameworks for connected vehicle modeling.

Equipment:

  • High-performance computing clusters
  • Multiple simulation software licenses
  • Real-time visualization systems
  • Data analysis workstations

V2X Communication Lab

Dedicated laboratory for testing vehicle-to-everything communication protocols and developing connected vehicle applications.

Equipment:

  • DSRC communication units
  • 5G communication modules
  • Network testing equipment
  • Protocol analyzers

Data Analytics Center

Big data processing and analytics infrastructure for handling large-scale transportation datasets and machine learning applications.

Capabilities:

  • Large-scale data storage systems
  • Machine learning frameworks
  • Real-time data processing
  • Cloud computing resources

Vehicle Testing Platform

Mobile testing platform for real-world validation of connected vehicle technologies and field data collection.

Features:

  • Instrumented test vehicles
  • GPS and sensor systems
  • Communication equipment
  • Data logging systems

Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) Demonstrator

Advanced demonstration facility for vehicle-to-grid integration, enabling research on bidirectional energy flow between electric vehicles and the power grid.

Features:

  • Bidirectional charging infrastructure
  • Grid integration testing systems
  • Energy management platforms
  • Real-time monitoring equipment

Grid Integration Laboratory

Specialized laboratory for studying the integration of transportation systems with electrical grid infrastructure and smart energy management.

Capabilities:

  • Smart grid simulation platforms
  • Energy storage systems
  • Load balancing equipment
  • Grid stability analysis tools

Research Focus Areas

Our comprehensive research program covers multiple aspects of connected and automated vehicle systems.

V2X Communication

Developing robust communication protocols for vehicle-to-vehicle, vehicle-to-infrastructure, and vehicle-to-pedestrian interactions.

DSRC Protocols 5G Networks Edge Computing Latency Optimization

Traffic Flow Optimization

Advanced algorithms for optimizing traffic flow in mixed environments with connected and conventional vehicles.

Signal Control Adaptive Systems Congestion Management Route Optimization

Safety Systems

Developing safety-critical applications for connected vehicles to prevent accidents and protect vulnerable road users.

Collision Avoidance Emergency Response Pedestrian Safety Weather Adaptation

AI & Machine Learning

Applying artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to enhance connected vehicle decision-making and prediction capabilities.

Predictive Modeling Deep Learning Behavior Analysis Real-time Processing
